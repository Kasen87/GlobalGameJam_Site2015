The Global Game Jam

Innovation. Collaboration. Experimentation.

What is it?

Participants at Blue1647 work concurrently with developers around the globe; we rally around a central theme, and then have 48 hours to create a game.

Who can participate?

Although having computer skills is helpful, code experience is not necessary. Designers, developers, artists and anyone is welcome to try their hand at making a game during the GGJ.

When and Where?

Blue1647 Tech Innovation Center
1647 S. Blue Island Ave, Chicago IL 60608
January 23 - 25, 2015

Is there an age limit?

Nope!
However, if you're under 17 you will need a parent's or guardian's signature on a permission slip before jamming!

Food and Drink

We'll have snacks available during the jam sessions but there are many local places for full meals.
Some are even open 24 hours a day!

On-site Security

We'll have staff on-site during the entire jam and we generally lock the outer doors after 11:00pm for added safety.
